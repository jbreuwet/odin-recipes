Odin's Homemade Meatballs

These delicious meatballs made by Odin himself are quick and easy to make for the family when your'e in a pinch for time or effort.

These meatballs can go from the fridge to table ready in under 30 minutes!

Ingredeints

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • large egg
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • tbsp fresh chopped basil
  • 1 tbsp fresh chopped oregano
  • 1 tbsp fresh chopped parsley
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Step-by-Step

  1. Allow ground beef to come to room temperature before mixing.
  2. Thouroughly mix all ingredients into bowl.
  3. Take take the mixed ground beef and form into balls about 1 inch in diameter.
  4. Place onto baking sheet lined with aluminum foil
  5. Bake on 400 degrees F for 20 minutes
  6. Serve!
